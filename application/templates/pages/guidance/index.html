We’re working with 73 local planning authorities (LPAs) through the Open Digital Planning community to help them provide data to our platform. There are four steps to follow:

  • prepare data to the specifications
  • use our service to check your data conforms to specifications
  • publish data on your local authority website
  • keep your data up-to-date

Using our service

We’re making improvements to our service which will make it simpler and easier for LPAs to provide and maintain data for planning and housing.

Streamlined data provision and progress tracking

Our step-by-step process guides you with real-time feedback, helping you track progress and provide data confidently.

Guided data mapping

Easily map your data headings to the specifications, using real examples from your datasets.

Actionable data validation and error resolution

Get clear instructions to resolve problems with your datasets, making it easier to conform to technical specifications.
